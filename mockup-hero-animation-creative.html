creative experiments

Five hero animations that don't look like diagrams.

The earlier options were all variations on "nodes with packets between them." These five step outside that pattern entirely — different visual metaphors, different storytelling approaches.

Concept 01 · The Consolidation

Tangle on the left, order on the right

Left side: six chaotic, overlapping integration lines (representing the "stitched-together" reality most banks live with). Center: EBB Gateway. Right: those same connections emerge as six clean, parallel, ordered lines.

What it tells: EBB takes integration chaos and consolidates it. The most direct visualization of EBB's actual value proposition. Doesn't need to enumerate operators — the contrast does the work.
Concept 02 · Kinetic Headline

The animation IS the headline

No diagram on the right side at all. Instead, one word in the hero headline animates — cycling through real operator names every 2 seconds. The headline itself shows the breadth.

What it tells: "every operator" is literal — the word shifts to show each one. Treats hero like editorial typography rather than infographic. Most minimalist; relies entirely on the text doing the work.

Concept 03 · The Pulse

EBB as the heartbeat of the bank's payments

A single horizontal ECG-style line runs across the hero. Spikes pulse at irregular but rhythmic intervals — like a heartbeat. EBB sits in the center as the source. Echoes the §2 problem section's ECG without competing.

What it tells: EBB is alive, working, processing — like vital signs. Calm but never static. Sophisticated; reads as "instrument panel" not "diagram."
Concept 04 · The Map

Africa in motion — payments lighting up across markets

An abstract silhouette of Africa fills the right side. Cities and corridors pulse with payment activity. Routes traced in crimson dashed lines suggest cross-border flow. EBB sits as the central pin.

What it tells: EBB is geographically real, operating across the continent. Distinct from any "diagram" — feels like a live operations dashboard. Banker recognises it as actual scale.
Concept 05 · Flow State

A continuous river of transactions through EBB

A dense stream of tiny particles flows continuously from left to right, passing through EBB at the center. Some particles turn crimson briefly as they cross EBB (representing successful processing). Like watching water through a turbine.

What it tells: Volume. Continuity. Transactions never stop. EBB is the conduit, not the destination. Mesmerizing rather than informational.
My honest top picks: Concept 01 (The Consolidation) for strategic clarity · Concept 03 (The Pulse) for editorial sophistication · Concept 05 (Flow State) for sheer mesmerism.
