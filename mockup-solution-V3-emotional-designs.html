D1 Baseline · 2×2 grid with crimson left rule Current canonical: white cards in a 2×2 grid, crimson 3px left rule on each. Clean, restrained.
D2 Horizontal 4-column strip All four themes in a single row with circular badge numbers. Tighter, more scannable. Risk: cards get narrow.
D3 Vertical editorial column · magazine spread 4 themes stacked vertically with large italic numerals. Slows the eye down; most magazine-like.
D4 Pull-quote treatment · tagline dominates Each theme's emotional tagline is the dominant element, set as a serif italic pull-quote. Theme name becomes the eyebrow.
D5 Asymmetric hero · one dominant + three supporting Peace of Mind takes 2/3 width as a dark navy hero card. Remaining 3 stack in the right third. Visual focal point.
D6 Two-column split · theme | capabilities Each row: large theme name + tagline on the left, capability bullets on the right. Clear emotional/technical dichotomy.
D7 2×2 grid · alternating cream/white 2×2 with alternating fill colors for visual rhythm. Cards feel paired diagonally.
D8 Manifesto · pure typography, no cards Just typographic hierarchy: large crimson italic theme name, supporting line, fine-print capabilities. Most editorial.
D9 Cards with circular crimson badge numerals 2×2 with prominent crimson circular badges floating on each card. Soft shadow elevation.
D10 Line-divided · crimson rules, no cards 4 themes stacked, separated by crimson horizontal rules. Two-column inside each (theme on left, capabilities on right).
