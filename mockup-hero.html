02 · the recurring pattern

Your payment platform works — until it doesn't.

  • — your customers see before your dashboards do.
  • — that fail silently for hours.
  • — you still do in spreadsheets every month-end.
  • — that consume your team for a week, every quarter.
  • — that take days to investigate across different teams.
  • — that drag on for more than six months across different teams.
  • — at midnight because daytime is too risky.
  • — that grows with every operator you add.
  • — bolted on after every new market.
  • — engineering headcount spent sustaining integration infrastructure, reconciliation operations, and failure recovery — instead of building revenue-generating products.
